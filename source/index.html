My Name is Andrew.

I am an upcoming M.A.Sc. graduate in civil engineering, looking to begin a career at a structural engineering consulting firm. I bring over two years of industry experience which includes structural wind engineering, land development, concrete manufacturing, and bridge rehabilitation. I am a driven individual who will use hard work and dedication to become a valuable asset to any consulting firm. I love the project management side of civil engineering and am looking to join a team of engineers who perform both the technical design, and coordination of assigned projects. I take a personable approach when communicating with clients, contractors, and fellow employees, and positively contribute to the social atmosphere of the office.
