A Brief Background

My interest in civil engineering stemmed from my love for LEGO as a young child. Ever since, I have been fascinated with how structures worked and why they were designed as they were. Fueling this passion is my attraction to project management which heavily characterizes consulting firms in our industry.

In my extra time, I have developed a strong interest in politics and public policy. I think the best way engineers can demonstrate our duty to society is to be directly involved in the decisions being made by our local government.
